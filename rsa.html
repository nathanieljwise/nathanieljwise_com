Message me securely at nathanieljwise@protonmail.com by following these steps:

  • Encrypt your message using my public key.
      openssl rsautl -encrypt -pubin -inkey public_key.pem -in unencrypted.txt -out myMessage.enc

  • Generate a hash of your encrypted message:
      openssl dgst -sha256 -binary myMessage.enc > myMessage_enc.hash

  • Send your encrypted message myMessage.enc and its hash myMessage_enc.hash to the secure email address above.

  • I'll hash the encrypted message I received from you, and verify my hash against yours.
      openssl dgst -sha256 -binary myMessage.enc > received_myMessage_enc.hash
      diff -s myMessage_enc.hash received_myMessage_enc.hash

  • Finally, I'll decrypt your message.
      openssl rsautl -decrypt -inkey private_key.pem -in myMessage.enc -out decrypted.txt
